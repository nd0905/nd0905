Past Work Projects and Learning

As I finish up my college career I have collected a good amount of experience and knowledge. I spent the last two summers working at a small cyber security focused managed service provider (email me if you would like to know more). During that time I spent many hours learning and growing. I took a number of courses and got some training in popular security products such as PaloAlto Next Generation Firewall, Trend Micro security suite, Symantec, and many others. Personal Projects

During my time at school I have taken a number of classes that give me a well rounded understanding of programming, security, operating systems, and more. I have taken a few information security classes where we learned the basics of how an attack happens and how to prevent these basic attacks. I have also taken a few development classes learning how to code in C, C++, Python, Java, Javascript, and some minor HTML. Doing all my personal projects has given me a good amount of experience and knowledge with linux, mac, and windows in different levels of understanding.
